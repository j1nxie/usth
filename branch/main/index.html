USTH Study Notes Emporium¶

This is an effort to condense, simplify and extend the contents of lectures for ICT majors at the University of Science and Technology of Hanoi. My goals are creating a clean, comprehensive and easy to understand version of lecture notes for everyone, with additional explanation if needed.

I found some lectures very long and sleepy, without much focus on the most important parts of the content. Thus, this serves as a repository of knowledge for subjects taught at USTH, but written in a way that everyone can gain something out of.
